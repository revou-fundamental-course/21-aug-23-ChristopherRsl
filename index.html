About Our School

Those who venture here—to learn, research, teach, work, and grow—join nearly four centuries of students and scholars in the pursuit of truth, knowledge, and a better world.

Mission

Harvard University's mission is rooted in the advancement of knowledge, wisdom, and innovation through rigorous research, dedicated teaching, and impactful public service. With an unwavering commitment to excellence and the highest standards of scholarship, Harvard aims to empower students to become leaders equipped with critical thinking skills and informed judgment. The university's diverse academic community seeks to prepare individuals for a purposeful life, meaningful work, and influential leadership across various sectors.

Vission

Harvard University envisions itself as a global leader in education, setting the standard for nurturing the leaders of the 21st century. The university strives to establish an exceptional environment that fosters unparalleled learning experiences, transformative discoveries, and intellectual growth. Through this vision, Harvard aims to educate and shape principled leaders who excel in academia, government, business, and nonprofit sectors. The institution's dedication to profound research, respect for diversity, and commitment to collaboration all contribute to its aspiration to create positive and lasting impacts on a global scale.
